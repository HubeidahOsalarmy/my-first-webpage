How to prepare egg fried rice

  1. Ensure you have your pre-cooked rice.
  2. Chop your veggies to your choice sizes.
  3. Slightly fry your veggies.
  4. Make your scrambled egg with little to no spice.
  5. Pack out your fried veggies into a bowl and add lil by lil as you add your precooked rice
  6. Mix thoroughly and also add your scrambled egg.
  7. Spice and garnish it up to your taste.
  8. Leave and let to simmer on low to medium heat.
  9. Serve with chilled orange juice.
